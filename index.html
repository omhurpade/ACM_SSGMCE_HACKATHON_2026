Tracks & Themes

Healthcare & Mental Well-Being

Develop technology solutions that address gaps in affordable healthcare access, early disease detection, mental health monitoring, remote patient support, or emergency response systems. Solutions should aim to improve accessibility, efficiency, or preventive care using data-driven or smart systems.

Education & Skill Development

Design digital platforms or tools that enhance learning outcomes, improve access to quality education in rural or underserved areas, enable personalized learning experiences, or bridge the gap between academic curriculum and industry-required skills.

Sustainable Development & Environment

Create scalable solutions that tackle environmental challenges such as waste management inefficiencies, energy optimization, water conservation, pollution monitoring, or climate awareness through smart monitoring and automation systems.

Smart Governance & Public Safety

Build systems that enhance transparency, efficiency, and responsiveness in public services, including grievance redressal platforms, traffic management systems, disaster alert mechanisms, or digital public service dashboards.

Agriculture & Rural Innovation

Develop smart agricultural solutions such as crop monitoring systems, soil health analytics, irrigation optimization, supply chain transparency tools, or farmer assistance platforms to increase productivity and reduce losses.

FinTech & Digital Inclusion

Design secure and accessible financial technology solutions that improve digital payments, fraud detection, financial literacy, microfinance access, or banking inclusion for underserved communities.
